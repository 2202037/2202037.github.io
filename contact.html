Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a healthcare provider?

Click on the "Register" button, select your provider type (Doctor, Hospital, Clinic, or Pharmacy), and fill out the registration form. Your application will be reviewed by our admin team.

How long does verification take?

Typically, verification takes 2-3 business days. You'll receive an email notification once your account is approved.

Is the platform free for patients?

Yes! Patients can register, search, and book appointments for free. You only pay the consultation fee to your chosen healthcare provider.

What payment methods are supported?

We support bKash and Nagad mobile banking services for secure online payments.
