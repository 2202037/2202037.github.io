About Ayur

Building a healthier Bangladesh through digital healthcare innovation

Our Mission

Ayur is committed to transforming healthcare accessibility in Bangladesh by creating a unified digital platform that connects patients with verified healthcare providers.

We believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare services, and technology can bridge the gap between patients and providers.

What We Do

  • ✓ Verify and onboard healthcare providers
  • ✓ Enable easy healthcare service discovery
  • ✓ Facilitate online appointment booking
  • ✓ Provide transparent reviews and ratings
  • ✓ Support emergency blood services

Why Choose Ayur?

🔒 Verified Providers

All healthcare providers undergo strict verification by our admin team before being listed on the platform.

🎯 Centralized Platform
